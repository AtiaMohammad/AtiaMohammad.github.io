MY FUTURE PLANS




I've never had any exact future plan till now. When I was in primary school I wanted to be a doctor, after that I wanted to be a chemist, then again I wanted to be a doctor. It was never fixed for me, but the thing that I've always wanted was to make my parents proud and to lead a happy life. But after getting admitted in UIU I have future plans. I want to finish my studies with a good CGPA and then I want to see my the proud smile of my parents while wearing the graduation hat. This is my ultimate future plan. To be a happy and good human being.
